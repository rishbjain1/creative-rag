# Image Tools

- **Higgsfield Soul 2.0** is the primary base-image generator. It reproduces real film optics from camera, lens, and stock vocabulary. Soul holds ONE character per image; with two or more, the extras inherit the main character's face.
- **NB Pro and GPT-2** are fixers, not base generators. Use them to swap a face or product onto a finished Soul image while keeping everything else identical.

# Video Models

- **Kling 3.0** is the daily driver, roughly 85% of shots: fast, cheap, best at camera moves, but morphs on heavy motion.
- **Seedance 2.0** is hero only: no morph, holds expression and lip-sync, and can do a 15s one-take.

# Camera Movement

- **Dolly in / push in** — creates intimacy, tension, and revelation; the world gets closer.
- **Dolly out / pull back** — creates isolation, release, and scale; the character shrinks as the world opens.
