# Film Stocks

- **Kodak Portra 400** — warm, rich golden tones, the classic editorial skin stock. The default day stock.
- **Cinestill 800T** — tungsten-balanced, teal/blue shadows, warm orange highlights. The night, indoor, neon and artificial-light stock.
- **Fuji Pro 400H** — slightly cool, creamy, soft, natural unprocessed skin for a restrained editorial feel.

# Lenses

- **14mm at f/8** — ultra-wide, deep focus, sharp foreground to background. Environmental scale when the location is the story.
- **35mm** — natural field of view, documentary, the viewer inside the scene.
- **85mm f/1.4** — flattering compression for close-ups and portraits, shallow depth of field that separates the subject from the background.
